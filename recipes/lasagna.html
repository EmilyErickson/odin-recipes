Lasagna

Description

Absolute best lasagna recipe ever!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Ground Beef
  • 1 pound Italian Sausage
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 cans Whole Tomatoes
  • 2 cans Tomato Paste
  • 2 Tablespoons Italian Seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 3 cups Cottage Cheese
  • 2 Whole Eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 Tablespoons Parsley
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 pound Sliced Mozzerella Cheese
  • 1 package Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of water to a boil
  2. In a large skillet or saucepan, combine ground beef, sausage, and garlic.
  3. Cook over medium-high heat until browned. Drain half the fat.
  4. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil and salt. After adding the tomatoes, the sauce mixture should simmer for 45 minutes while you are working on the other steps.
  5. In a medium bowl, mix cottage cheese, beaten eggs, grated Parmesan, 2 more tablespoons parsley, and 1 more teaspoon salt.
  6. Stir together well. Set aside. Cook lasagna until “al dente” (not overly cooked).
  7. Arrange 4 cooked lasagna noodles in the bottom of a baking pan, overlapping if necessary.
  8. Spoon half the cottage cheese mixture over the noodles. Spread evenly.
  9. Cover cottage cheese with a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  10. Spoon a little less than half the meat/sauce mixture over the top.
  11. Repeat, ending with meat/sauce mixture. Sprinkle top generously with extra Parmesan.
  12. Bake immediately at 350-degree oven for about an hour, or until top is hot and bubbly.
