Sourdough Crackers

Description

Sourdough discard crackers are thin, crispy and have that amazing tang that you can only get with real sourdough

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Sourdough Starter Discard
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 teaspoons Dried Herbs

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (162°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Melt the butter in a mixing bowl and let cool
  3. Weigh the sourdough discard, dried herbs, and salt into the bowl of melted butter and mix thoroughly until well combined.
  4. Use an off-set spatula to spread the mixture in a thin, even layer onto the parchment paper.
  5. Bake for ten minutes. Remove from over and score the crackers. Bake for and additional 20-50 minutes or until the crackers are golden brown.
  6. Let cool completely before breaking into squares.
